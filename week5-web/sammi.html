About me

Hi! My name is Sammi Chen, and I am an upcoming junior at George Washington High School in SF. I have lived here my whole life, and I have never been exposed to computer science before this program.

Field Trips

Pinterest June 24, 2016

On June 24, the Adobe Girls Who Code combined with Moody's Girls Who Code and went to Pinterest, on 7th and Brannan street in SF. We had a tour through the building, ate their amazing food, listened to their speakers, had lunch with some interns and employees, and participated in workshops.

Twitter July 13, 2016

On July 13, most of the Girls Who Code programs met up at Twitter headquarters, here in San Francisco. We didn't have a tour, but we listened to the director of GWC, the CEO of GWC, and the CEO of Twitter. They talked about their career paths and past experiences, and we had a chance to ask them questions.
